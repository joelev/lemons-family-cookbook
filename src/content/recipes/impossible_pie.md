---
title: "Impossible Pie"
category: cakes-pies-frostings
---

- 2 cups milk
- 4 eggs
- 1/4 cup butter or margarine
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1/2 cup honey or sorgum
- 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg or cinnamon
- 1 cup unsweetened coconut

Combine all ingredients in a blender. Blent 20-30 seconds. Pour into greased 9" pie pan. Bake 350 degrees for 45 min. Makes own crust and delicious filling.
