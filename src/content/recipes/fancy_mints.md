---
title: "Fancy Mints"
category: candies-cookies-confections
---

- 1 egg white
- 1 stick Oleo
- 1 box powdered sugar
- mint flavoring
- cake coloring (any color you want)

Mix and beat the ingredients until they lose grainy texture. Put in cake decorator and use any design you want -- on wax paper. We made these for Connie's wedding. Makes 800 to 1,000 mints.
