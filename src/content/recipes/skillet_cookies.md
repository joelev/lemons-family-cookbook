---
title: "Skillet Cookies"
category: candies-cookies-confections
---

- 1 stick butter
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 8 oz. box dates, chopped
- 1 cup nuts (pecans or English walnuts)
- 2 cups rice krispies
- Angel flake coconut (to roll in)

Mix butter, sugar, eggs, and dates. Cook in skillet for 15 minutes. Add nuts, dates, & krispies. Cool, roll in balls, then roll in coconut. Another Jessie Glass treat.
