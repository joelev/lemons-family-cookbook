---
title: "Old Fashioned Cocoa Fudge"
category: candies-cookies-confections
---

- 2 cups sugar
- 1/3 cup cocoa
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 cup milk
- 2 Tablespoons white syrup
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 Tablespoons butter or margerine
- 1/2 cup chopped nuts (English walnuts)

Mix dry ingredients in skillet. Gradually add milk & syrup. Boil to soft ball stage. Remove from heat, add butter, vanilla & nuts. Cool to lukewarm without stirring. Beat till thick and no longer glossy. Pour into buttered pan to set.
