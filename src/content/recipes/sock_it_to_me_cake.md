---
title: "Sock It To Me Cake"
category: cakes-pies-frostings
---

- 1 box Duncan Hines butter cake mix.
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup cooking oil
- 1 cup dairy sour cream
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 4 eggs -- one at a time
- 1 cup nuts (pecans or English walnuts)

Mix above ingredients together and beat well. Pour half of batter into a well greased and floured bundt pan. Mix together 2 Tablespoons brown sugar, and 2 teaspoons cinnamon. Sprinkle over batter in pan. Pour in rest of batter and bake 55 min. at 325 degrees. Turn out of pan while hot and glaze with CREAMY ICING.
