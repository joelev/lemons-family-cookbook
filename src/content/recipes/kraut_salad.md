---
title: "Kraut Salad"
category: salads
story: "This is Grandpa Orville's favorite."
---

- 1 cup chopped kraut
- 1 green pepper, chopped
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 1/2 cup celery, chopped
- 1 small can chopped pimentos
- 1/2 cup vinegar
- 2/3 cup sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

Stir all together and let set in refrigerator overnight.
