---
title: "Jesse Glass Cake"
category: cakes-pies-frostings
---

Cream together:

- 2 eggs
- 2 cups sugar

Add alternately:

- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons soda
- 1 teaspoon salt -- sifted together.
- 2 1/4 cups crushed pineapple with juice (#2 can)

Add:

- 2 Tablespoons cooking oil
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1/2 cup chopped pecans

This will make a foamy, fluffy mixture. Bake in 9 by 13 greased, floured pan at 350 degrees 30 -35 min. Cool cake and ice.
