---
title: "Marian's Peanut Butter Fudge"
category: candies-cookies-confections
---

- 2 cups sugar
- 3/4 cup milk
- 5 Tablespoons chunky peanut butter
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- pinch of salt (1/8 tsp)

Boil sugar & milk to hard ball stage. Let cool slightly, add rest of ingredients & beat till smooth. Pour out fast onto buttered pan -- it sets up quickly.

Marian Rice -- delicious.
