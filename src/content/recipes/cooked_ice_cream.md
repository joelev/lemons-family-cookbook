---
title: "Cooked Ice Cream"
category: candies-cookies-confections
story: "Cool, add cream & vanilla. Strain & freeze. This is for those who do not want the eggs cooked. Can substitue Eagle Brand or half & half for part of the milk to make richer."
---

- 7 cups scalded milk
- 2 1/2 tablespoons flour
- 2 1/2 cups sugar
- 3 eggs, slightly beaten
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 2 cups cream
- 3 tablespoons Watkins vanilla

Mix flour, salt, & sugar. Add slightly beaten eggs & milk gradually, beating to mix smoothly. Cook over low heat for 10 to 15 minutes, stirring constantly. Watch closely -- will scorch easily.
