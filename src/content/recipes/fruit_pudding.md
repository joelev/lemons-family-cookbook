---
title: "Fruit Pudding"
category: candies-cookies-confections
---

#1 Mix together:

- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 cup crushed pineapple or applesauce
- 1 teaspoon mixed spices

#2 Mix 1 tsp. soda in 3 TBS. buttermilk. Add soda and milk to first mixture. Stir in 6 TBS. melted margerine. Pour into greased, floured pan & bake 350 degrees about 25-30 minutes. Nuts, dates, raisins, etc may be added. Top with penuche icing or lemon sauce.
