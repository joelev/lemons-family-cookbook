---
title: "Chocolate Chip Cookies"
category: candies-cookies-confections
---

- 2/3 cup soft shortening
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup brown sugar (packed)
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup chopped nuts
- 1 package chocolate chips (6 oz.)

Mix shortening, sugars, egg & vanilla thoroughly. Measure sifted flour with other dry ingredients. Blend in and stir well. Mix in nuts & choc. chips. Drop rounded teaspoonfuls 2 in. apart on ungreased baking sheet. Bake 8 to 10 min. in 375 degrees oven. Cool slightly before removing from sheet.
