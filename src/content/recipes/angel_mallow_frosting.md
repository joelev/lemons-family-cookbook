---
title: "Angel Mallow Frosting"
category: candies-cookies-confections
---

- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1 egg white
- 1 Tablespoon water
- 1 cup marshmallow creme
- 1/4 teaspoon vanilla

Combine egg white, sugar, & water in top of double boiler. Beat with electric mixer until it forms soft peaks. Add marshmallow creme & beat until stiff peaks form. Stir in vanilla. Sprinkle coconut over top.
