---
title: "Italian Cream Cake"
category: cakes-pies-frostings
story: "Nadine Coleman of Memphis bakes this. It is a lot of trouble, but very good."
---

- 1 stick margerine
- 1/2 cup shortening
- 2 cups sugar
- 5 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1 cup chopped pecans
- 1 small can coconut (or 1 cup)
- 2 cups sifted flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 5 stiffly beaten egg whites

Cream margerine, shortening, and sugar. Add egg yolks. Beat. Add soda to the buttermilk, then add alternately with the flour to first mixture. Add salt, vanilla, coconut, and pecans. Beat until fluffy, then fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites. Bake in the tube pan (or spring form pan) in 350 degrees oven till done -- about 30 minutes. Ice with the cream cheese frosting.
