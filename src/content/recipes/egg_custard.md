---
title: "Egg Custard"
category: cakes-pies-frostings
---

Step 1

Scald (bring to simmer) 3 cups milk Step 2 Beat together until fluffy: 3 eggs 1/3 cup sugar 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg 1 teaspoon vanilla Step 3 Gradually pour scalded milk into egg mixture, stirring constantly. Pour into unbaked pie shell and bake at 350 degrees 30 to 40 minutes, or until center is just "set". Good.
