---
title: "Never Fail Divinity"
category: candies-cookies-confections
---

- 2 1/2 cups white sugar
- 2 1/2 cup light corn syrup
- 1/2 cup boiling water
- 2 egg whites
- 1/2 teaspoon cream of tarter
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 3/4 cups English walnuts

Beat egg whites & cream of tarter in large bowl until stiff & set aside. Boil sugar, salt, syrup & water until it forms a hard bell when dropped in cold water. Add half of this boiling syrup to egg whites slowly, beating with mixer all the time. Place remaining syrup back on burner & boil another two minutes. Add this slowly to egg whites & add vanilla. Beat until it starts to set up, then add nuts & pour into greased pan.
