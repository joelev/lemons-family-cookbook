---
title: "Easiest Ever Peanut Butter Cookies"
category: candies-cookies-confections
---

- 1 cup crunchy peanut butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

Mix all together well. Form into 1 inch balls and place on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for 8-10 minutes. Remove carefully as they are very tender when warm.
