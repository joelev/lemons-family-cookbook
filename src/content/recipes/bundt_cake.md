---
title: "Bundt Cake"
category: cakes-pies-frostings
story: "This is Connie's recipe, and very good."
---

- 1 box yellow cake mix
- 1 box vanilla pudding
- 3/4 cup corn oil
- 3/4 cup cold water
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 teaspoon butter flavoring

Mix and set aside: 1/4 cup sugar, 1/2 cup pecans, & 2 teaspoons cinnamon. Grease & flour bundt pan well.

Pour 1/3 batter, sprinkle 1/2 cinnamon mixture, 1/3 more batter, rest of cin. mixture and the rest of the cake batter. Bake at 350 degrees for 45-50 min. Cool for 10 min., and top with mixture of: 1 cup p. sugar, 1/2 ts. vanilla, 1/2 ts. butter flavoring, 2 TS. milk or cream.
