---
title: "Chocolate Drop Cookies"
category: candies-cookies-confections
---

- 1/2 cup shortening
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup cocoa
- 3/4 cup buttermilk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 3/4 cups flour
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup chopped nuts or coconut if desired

Mix shortening, sugar, and egg. Stir in buttermilk and vanilla. Sift together the flour, cocoa, salt & soda. Stir into mixture and blend well. Mix in nuts and chill at least 1 hour. Heat oven to 400 degrees and drop rounded teaspoonfuls of dough 2 in. apart onto lightly greased baking sheet. Bake 8-10 minutes or until done.
