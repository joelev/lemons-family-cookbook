---
title: "Lemon Dessert"
category: candies-cookies-confections
---

- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup chopped nuts (English walnuts or pecans)
- 1 stick margerine, softened

Mix and press into a 9 x 13 baking dish. Bake 20 to 30 minutes at 350 degrees, then let cool.

- 1 8oz package cream cheese
- 2 large tubs of Cool Whip
- 3 small packages lemon instant pudding mix
- 1 Tablespoon lemon juice
- 3 cups milk

Soften cream cheese and mix with one tub of Cool Whip. Spread over baked & cooled crust. Mix pudding mix, lemon juice & milk together. Fold in the second tub of Cool Whip. Pour over all. Sprinkle chopped nuts on top. Very rich.
