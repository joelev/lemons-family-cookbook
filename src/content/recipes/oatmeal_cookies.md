title: "Oatmeal Cookies"
category: candies-cookies-confections
story: "This is the recipe I found that Connie used to bake a LOT. One of my favorite cookies."
---

- 3/4 cup soft shortening
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 1 egg
- 1/4 cup water
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 cup sifted flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- 3 cups uncooked oats

Place shortening, sugars, egg, water and vanilla in mixing bowl; beat thoroughly. Sift together flour, salt, & soda. Add to shortening mix, mixing well. Blend in oats and drop by teaspoonfuls onto greased cookie sheet. Bake in moderate oven (350 degrees) 12 to 15 minutes. For variety add nuts, raisins, chocolate chips or whatever to dough.
