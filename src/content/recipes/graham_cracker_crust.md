---
title: "Graham Cracker Crust"
category: cakes-pies-frostings
---

- 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs (18)
- 3 Tablespoons sugar (if wanted -- I don't)
- 1/3 cup butter, melted

Melt butter over low heat in pan. Add crumbs and sugar. Press into pan and bake 10 min. at 350 degrees. Cool and fill with creamy type filling.
