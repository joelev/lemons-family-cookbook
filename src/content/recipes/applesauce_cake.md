---
title: "Applesauce Cake"
category: cakes-pies-frostings
story: "I like Penuche Icing with this."
---

- 2 3/4 cups cake flour (or 2 1/2 regular)
- 2 cups sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 1/2 teaspoons soda
- 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
- 1 teaspoon WATKINS cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon each cloves & nutmeg
- 1/2 cup soft shortening
- 1 1/2 cups unsweetened applesauce
- 2 eggs (1/3 to 1/2 cup)
- 1/2 cup chopped pecans (or walnuts)
- 1 cup raisins, cut up

Heat over to 350 degrees. Blend dry ingredients in bowl. Add shortening, water and applesuace. Beat 2 min. Add eggs and beat 2 more minutes. Stir in nuts and raisins (I leave them out). Pour into 9 by 13 greased and floured pan and bake 45 to 50 min. 350 degrees.
