---
title: "Mina Lou's Ritz Cracker Pie"
category: cakes-pies-frostings
---

- 3 egg whites -- beat stiff
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

Mix well.

Add:

- 21 ground Ritz crackers
- 1 cup pecans

Mix well and put in greased pie plate. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 min. Chill and top with Dream Whip (will dry out on top if not topped with something).
