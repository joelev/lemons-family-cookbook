---
title: "Sweet & Sour Chicken"
category: main-dishes-meats-vegetables
---

Remove bones from 3 or 4 chicken breasts. Cut in bite sized pieces. Cover with water and 1/2 teaspoon salt and soak an hour or so. Drain, dip in mixture of: 1/2 cup flour, 1/2 ts salt & 1/2 ts pepper. Fry in hot oil until brown. Remove & drain well.

In saucepan mix:

- 1/4 cup vinegar
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup tomato catsup
- 1 TBS. soy sauce

Mix 2 TBS cornstarch into 1/4 cup water, add to other ingredients & stir over low heat till thick & is a clear red color. Add 1 cup pineapple chunks, 1/2 cup sliced green peppers, & 1 cup cooked carrots. Serve chicken on rice & pour sauce over. Sprinkle with a few chopped onions & cashews if you like.
