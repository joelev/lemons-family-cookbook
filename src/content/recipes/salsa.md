---
title: "Salsa"
category: main-dishes-meats-vegetables
---

- 1 can tomatoes, drained & chopped
- 1 cup finely chopped onion
- 1 can (4 oz) green chilies
- 2 teaspoons finely chopped canned jalapena
- 1 clove crushed garlic (if you like)
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- dash of pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon chili powder

Mix all ingredients in medium bowl & let stand a few minutes to develop flavor.
