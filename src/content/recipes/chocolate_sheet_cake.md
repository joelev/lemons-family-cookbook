---
title: "Chocolate Sheet Cake"
category: cakes-pies-frostings
story: "This is Vivian King's recipe, and very good."
---

- 2 cups sugar
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

Sift together in mixing bowl.

- 1/2 cup oil
- 1 stick margerine
- 4 Tablespoons cocoa
- 1 cup water

Bring to rapid boil and pour over dry ingredients.

- 1/2 cup buttermilk
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

Add to mixture in order, and mix well.

Bake in greased cookie sheet 15-20 min. Frost immediately (while hot) with the following:

- 1/2 cup (1 stick) margerine
- 6 Tablespoons buttermilk
- 4 Tablespoons cocoa

Mix together, and bring to boil. Stir in:

- 1 box powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 cup nuts
