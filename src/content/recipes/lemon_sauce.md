---
title: "Lemon Sauce"
category: candies-cookies-confections
---

- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons corn starch
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon grated lemon rind
- 1 cup water
- 1/4 cup butter or margerine

Mix sugar and corn startch in sauce pan. Gradually stir in the water, and boil 1 minute, stirring constantly. Stir in butter, lemon juice & rind. Pour over pudding and serve hot -- will be thin. May susbstitute vanilla or rum flavoring for the lemon.
