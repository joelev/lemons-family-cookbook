---
title: "Penuche Icing"
category: cakes-pies-frostings
---

- 1/2 cup butter or margerine
- 1 cup (packed) brown sugar
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1 3/4 to 2 cups powdered sugar

Melt butter in sauce-pan. Stir in brown sugar. Stir and boil over low heat 2 min. Stir in milk. Bring to boil, stirring constantly. Cool to lukewarm. Gradually stir in powdered sugar until thick enough to spread.
