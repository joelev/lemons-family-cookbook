---
title: "Cauliflower and Broccoli Salad"
category: salads
story: "I think this is just as good if you use one of the creamy dressings, instead of the cream & Miracle Whip -- I like Green Goddess. Wayne likes this marinated in Italian dressing."
---

- 2 cups coarsely chopped cauliflower
- 2 cups coarsely chopped broccoli
- 2 cups chopped celery
- 3 or 4 chopped green onions
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- black pepper to taste
- 1 cup sweet cream
- 3 tablespoons Miracle Whip

Blend all ingredients and refrigerate before serving.
