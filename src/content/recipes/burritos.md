---
title: "Burritos"
category: main-dishes-meats-vegetables
---

Preheat oven to 350 degrees

In heavy skillet saute 1/4 cup onion in 2 TBS butter till brown. Remove from heat & add 1/2 cup green chilies, undrained, and 2 cups pinto beans (or can use can of refrieds).

Mix this well & spread 1/4 cup over each flour tortilla. Put a layer of cooked, seasoned hamburger meat on this, then top with layer of shredded Monterey Jack cheese & 2 TBS salsa.

Fold over burritos in center & place in 2 qts. baking dish, face down & bake 15 minutes.
