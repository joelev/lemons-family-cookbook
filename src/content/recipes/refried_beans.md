---
title: "Refried Beans"
category: main-dishes-meats-vegetables
---

Boil 1 lb. pinto beans in water until done. Drain & reserve liquid. Fry 8 slices bacon in large skillet till brown. Drain & crumble.

In bacon drippings in skillet, saute 1/4 cup chopped onion, 1/4 cup green pepper, 1 tsp. salt, 1 tsp. chili powder & cook over medium heat, stirring and mashing beans until all are mashed. Add enough bean juice to make a creamy mixture.

Pour into baking dish & top with 1/4 cup Cheddar cheese shredded & the crumbled bacon. Heat.
