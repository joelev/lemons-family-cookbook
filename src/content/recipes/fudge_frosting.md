---
title: "Fudge Frosting"
category: cakes-pies-frostings
---

- 2 cups sugar
- 6 Tablespoons cocoa
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon white syrup
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 3 Tablespoons butter

Cook together sugar, cocoa, milk, syrup, and salt till it forms a firm (not hard) ball. Add the vanilla and butter. Beat till thick and glossy. Pour over cake. Rich.
