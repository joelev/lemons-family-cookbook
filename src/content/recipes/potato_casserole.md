---
title: "Potato Casserole"
category: main-dishes-meats-vegetables
---

- 4 cups sliced potatoes
- 1 can mushroom soup
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1 3 oz. pkg. cream cheese
- 1/2 cup cheese (Velveta)

Put sliced potatoes in baking dish. Heat mushroom soup, cut up cream cheese & milk until dissolved, but don't boil. Add cheese to sliced potatoes & pour the heated mixture over potatoes & cheese. Sprinkle with pepper or paprika.

Bake at 350 degrees until done.
