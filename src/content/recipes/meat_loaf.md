---
title: "Meat Loaf"
category: main-dishes-meats-vegetables
story: "This was Lowell Kahler's favorite meat dish when we used to get together with Timmy, & Bobo, Ruby & Gary. I don't make it much any more (Wayne doesn't care for it) but it is a good meat loaf recipe."
---

- 1 pound lean hamburger meat
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 cup chopped onion
- 1/4 cup tomato catsup (Brooks)
- 1 cup cooked oatmeal
- 1 cup water

Mix together well, shape into loaf and place into well-greased pan. Bake at 350 degrees till done -- approximately 1 hour.
