---
title: "Lasagna, Quick & Easy"
category: main-dishes-meats-vegetables
---

- 1/2 teaspoon oil
- 1 small onion, diced
- 1 lb. ground beef
- 1 tablespoon flaked parsley (optional)
- 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
- 1/2 cup low fat mozzarella cheese
- 1 small carton cottage cheese
- 1 small can tomatoes or tomato sauce
- 1 package, spaghetti seasoning
- 1/2 package lasagna noodles

Cook the noodles in water until done. Saute onion in oil, add ground beef and cook until done. Add seasonings to beef.

In long baking dish place a layer of noodles. Spread a thin layer of beef mixture, then a layer of cottage cheese & shredded cheeses. Repeat this one time, then pour over all the tomatoes or sauce (may need to add a little water if you use sauce).

Top with another layer of the noodles & bake in 375 degrees oven until the cheese melts & top noodles are done. Add or substitute whatever you have or like.
