title: "Date Pudding"
category: candies-cookies-confections
story: "Mix together and pour over batter in pan -- do not stir. Bake 30 minutes in medium (350 degrees) oven. This is very good -- the one Grandma Lemons used to make."
---

Part 1

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 2/3 cup milk
- 1 cup chopped dates
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

Mix together & spread in greased pan.

Part 2

- 1 Tablespoon butter
- 1 cup nutmeats (pecans)
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 1/4 cup boiling water
