---
title: "Cream Pies"
category: cakes-pies-frostings
---

Bake crust first.

- 1/3 cup flour
- 2/3 cup sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 2 cups milk
- 3 well beaten egg yolks
- 1 Tablespoon butter or margerine
- 1/2 teaspoon WATKINS vanilla

Sift dry ingredients together in heavy pan or skillet. Over low heat, gradually blend in, and stir constantly, 2 cups milk. When mixture begins to thicken, spoon out 3 Tablespoons of hot mixture into 3 well beaten egg yolks. When blended, return to skillet and cook until thick. Remove from ehat, and add 1 Tablespoon butter, and 1/2 ts. Watkins vanilla -- any other kind, use 1.

For COCONUT cream, stir 1 cup coconut into hot mixture.

For BANANA cream, line crust with sliced bananas pour in filling, and top with another layer of bananas.

For CHOCOLATE pie use 1 cup sugar, and 1/4 cup cocoa.

For PINEAPPLE cream, use only 1 3/4 cups milk, and add 1 cup crushed pineapple.

Which-ever flavor you choose, pour into baked crust, and top with meringue.
