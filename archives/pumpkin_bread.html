Pumpkin Bread

  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon nutmeg

Sift together above ingredients.

Beat together in large bowl:

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups pumpkin (home canned or frozen)
  • 1 cup oil
  • 2/3 cup water

Stir in dry ingredients and mix well. Bake in well greased, floured pan for 1 hour at 350°, or until done.

I like this, and I usually toss a few pecans in unless Kirk is here. You can cut back about half on the sugar and eggs if you don't want it quite so rich, and it will still be good -- just not as light.
