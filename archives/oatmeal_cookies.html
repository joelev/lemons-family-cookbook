Oatmeal Cookies

  • ¾ cup soft shortening
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup sifted flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon soda
  • 3 cups uncooked oats

Place shortening, sugars, egg, water and vanilla in mixing bowl; beat thoroughly. Sift together flour, salt, & soda. Add to shortening mix, mixing well. Blend in oats and drop by teaspoonfuls onto greased cookie sheet. Bake in moderate oven (350°) 12 to 15 minutes. For variety add nuts, raisins, chocolate chips or whatever to dough.

This is the recipe I found that Connie used to bake a LOT. One of my favorite cookies.
